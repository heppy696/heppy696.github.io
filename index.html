Health and Wellness

Wellness is an active process of becoming aware of and making choices toward a healthy and fulfilling life. Wellness is more than being free from illness, it is a dynamic process of change and growth.

Health is a state of complete physical, mental and social well-being, and not merely the absence of disease or infirmity. Health is a resource for everyday life, not the objective of living.

Good health is not just the absence of disease or infirmity. It is a state of physical, mental, and social well-being. Having good health means being able to lead a full life with energy and happiness.
